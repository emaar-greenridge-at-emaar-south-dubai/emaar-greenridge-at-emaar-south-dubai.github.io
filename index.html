Greenridge at Emaar South – Flexible Payment Plan

Emaar understands the importance of flexibility when it comes to purchasing your dream home, which is why Greenridge at Emaar South offers an attractive and straightforward payment plan tailored to meet your financial needs. Whether you're an investor or a homeowner, this payment plan is designed to make homeownership easy and hassle-free.

Here’s a breakdown of the Greenridge at Emaar South Dubai payment plan:

Initial Deposit

  • 10% of the total property value upon booking.

During Construction

  • 10% payment upon 3 months after booking.
  • 10% payment upon 6 months after booking.
  • 10% payment upon 9 months after booking.
  • 10% payment upon 12 months after booking.

On Handover

  • 50% final payment upon the handover of your townhouse.

This phased payment structure ensures that you can comfortably plan your finances over time, making it easier for you to secure your place in this premium development. With this manageable payment schedule, owning a luxurious townhouse in Greenridge at Emaar South Dubai is within reach for homeowners and investors alike.
